Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic and beloved treat that features a buttery, golden-brown cookie dough studded with morsels of rich, semi-sweet chocolate. These cookies have a slightly crisp exterior and a soft, chewy interior, making them irresistible. When freshly baked, the chocolate chips are melty and gooey, adding to the indulgent experience.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Baking: 8-10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour, baking soda and salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, cream together softened butter and both brown and white granulated sugars until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Fold in the semi-sweet chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough balls onto a parchment-lined baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 8-10 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
